About us

At Thakur Bicycle, we believe that cycling is more than just a ride – it is an experience of freedom, fitness, and fun. Our journey started with a simple mission: to design bicycles that inspire people to stay active, explore new horizons, and enjoy every moment on two wheels. Over the years, we have worked tirelessly to combine technology, creativity, and passion to deliver cycles that stand out in style, durability, and performance.

Our bicycles are crafted for everyone – from kids taking their first ride, to daily commuters looking for comfort, to adventure lovers seeking thrill on mountain trails. Every model is built with precision engineering, high-quality materials, and modern designs to ensure smooth rides and long-lasting performance. Whether it’s city roads, off-road paths, or fitness rides, Thakur Bicycle provides the perfect companion for every journey.

With thousands of happy customers and a strong dealer network, Thakur Bicycle has become a trusted name in the cycling industry. We are not just building bicycles; we are building a community of riders who share the joy and excitement of cycling. Our commitment extends beyond products—we provide excellent customer support, after-sale services, and a promise of reliability that makes us stand apart.

At Thakur Bicycle, our vision is to encourage healthier lifestyles and promote eco-friendly transportation. Cycling is the future, and we want every rider to be a part of this change. When you choose Thakur Bicycle, you choose innovation, passion, and unforgettable experiences on every ride. Together, let’s pedal towards a healthier, greener, and happier world.
